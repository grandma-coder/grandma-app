10

One icon line.

A single thin, rounded stroke — delicate and unified, no per-behaviour variation. They sit in ink over any field. Below the core line, the full app roster — every glyph the screens use — ported into the same delicate stroke.

11

The mark, diffused.

The heart-eye survives the move — redrawn as one wobbly hairline. A holds the wash inside the heart-iris; B keeps the drawing pure ink and lets one soft bloom glow behind it. The tint follows the journey; ink never tints.

A · gradient iris
B · halo bloom

She stays alive in the hero mark — the same watchful motion as always: a slow blink every few seconds, the heart-iris keeping a soft heartbeat. In dense specimen grids and small placements the mark rests still. Motion is blink + beat by default, reducible to blink-only or heartbeat-only, and always yields to reduced-motion.

12

The app icon.

A — the journey field itself becomes the tile: grainy diffuse gradient, hairline mark in ink. B — quiet paper tile with the gradient-iris mark, for monochrome shelves and dark.

14

Pillar stickers.

The 24 pillar cards, redrawn in the sticker hand. Each pillar’s wash is pulled from its journey field — used at ~32px in pillar grids, ~96px in detail heroes.

15

Forty weeks.

“Baby is the size of a…” — poppy seed to small pumpkin, every week drawn in the same wobbly line. The doodle grows with the week and the wash deepens by trimester: gold → blush → plum.

Or the soft-crayon register — each food a filled pastel shape with a grainy pencil edge and a friendly little face (dot eyes, a smile, rosy cheeks). Every week reads instantly as its own produce, cheerful on cream and dark alike.
