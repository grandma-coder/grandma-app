Each dot = one week (40 total). Pick the interaction model that feels right.

⭐ RECOMMENDED

A · Rotatable Ring

40 dots in a full circle, color-coded by trimester. Drag to spin — it rotates like a dial, snapping each week to the top. The current week pulses. Tapping any dot jumps to it. Feels playful and satisfying.

ALTERNATIVE

B · Swipe Arc

A half-circle arc at the top, dots sweep left–right. Swipe along the arc to move between weeks. Week card snaps below. Easier one-handed use, more screen space for details.
