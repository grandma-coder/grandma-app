Calendar system summary
🗓️

Month View

Trimester-colored grid. Dots show log types per day (purple = mood/symptoms, green = routines done, yellow = appointment). Tap any day → see logged entries + today's routine checklist at bottom.

📋

Week Timeline

7-day strip at top. "Still to log today" purple strip shows pending daily routines. Below: color-coded timeline of all entries for selected day — mood, routines, symptoms, weight, kicks.

🛤️

Journey (weeks 4–40)

Full pregnancy arc. Past weeks show baby size + your log summary (how many entries). Current week highlighted. Future weeks faded. Swipeable through the whole pregnancy.

🏥

Appointments View

Vertical timeline of all standard pregnancy checkpoints (pre-seeded) + your custom ones. Done → green. Next → amber with prep note. Upcoming → faded. Tap done appointment to add results.

🔁

Daily Routines (10 types)

Vitamins, water, weight, kicks, mood, symptoms, sleep, exercise, nutrition, kegels. Show as "to log" strip in Month + Week views. Auto-seeded as pending each day. Mark done with one tap.

📝

Event Logs (7 types)

Appointment, exam result, ultrasound, contraction timer, medicine/vaccine, birth prep task, nesting task. Added via FAB or quick-log. Appear as timeline entries + feed analytics.
